A warm-white surface with soft border and gentle shadow — the base container for panels and content blocks.

```jsx
<Card padding="lg" interactive>
  <h3>Organic Balloon Garland</h3>
  <p>Hand-built to your colours.</p>
</Card>
<Card tone="ink" padding="lg">Featured section</Card>
```

`tone`: `card` (warm white) · `soft` (blush) · `shell` (sand) · `ink` (dark plum). `padding`: `none`/`sm`/`md`/`lg`. `interactive` adds a hover lift for clickable cards.
